Problem Statement: Delay Avoidance

People need a better way to accurately track how long it will take them to get to class. This includes expected stoplight and crosswalk traffic.

Affinity diagram: Delay Avoidance App

In our affinity diagram for an app to help people get to class on time, we found our categories to be the design of the app, how the app helps users to navigate, what delays and other circumstances the app would notify you of, personal preference as to route and timing of arrival, and customization as to the user's needs of the app.
